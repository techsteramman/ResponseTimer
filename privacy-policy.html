ResponseTimer Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

ResponseTimer ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension collects, uses, and protects your data.

Data Collection and Usage

Our extension collects and processes the following data:

  • Email timestamps from your Gmail threads
  • Email addresses of recipients in tracked threads
  • Response time calculations

All data processing occurs locally within your browser. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Tracked thread information
  • Recipient response time statistics
  • User preferences

Gmail Integration

The extension requires access to Gmail to:

  • Read email timestamps for response time calculations
  • Identify email recipients
  • Add tracking functionality to the Gmail interface

We do not read, store, or process the content of your emails.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete any tracked data at any time through the extension's interface. All data is automatically removed when you uninstall the extension.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools. All functionality is self-contained within the browser extension.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
