ResponseTimer Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

Introduction

ResponseTimer ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension collects, uses, and protects your data.

Data Collection and Usage

Our extension collects and processes the following data:

  • Your Gmail address (provided during signup)
  • Email timestamps from your Gmail threads
  • Email addresses of recipients in tracked threads
  • Response time calculations
Important: All email response time data is processed and stored locally in your browser. We do not have access to your email content or response time data.

Email Collection and Mailing List

When you sign up to use ResponseTimer, your email address is:

  • Stored locally in your browser for extension functionality
  • Added to our mailing list (managed by Mailchimp) to receive important updates about the extension

You can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails.

Data Storage

We store:

  • Locally in your browser:
    • Your Gmail address
    • Tracked thread information
    • Response time calculations
    • User preferences
  • In our mailing list:
    • Your email address
    • Subscription status

Gmail Integration

The extension requires access to Gmail to:

  • Read email timestamps for response time calculations
  • Identify email recipients
  • Add tracking functionality to the Gmail interface

We do not read, store, or transmit the content of your emails.

Third-Party Services

We use Mailchimp to manage our mailing list. By providing your email address, you acknowledge that the information you provide will be transferred to Mailchimp for processing in accordance with their Privacy Policy and Terms.

Data Deletion

You can:

  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Unsubscribe from our mailing list using the link in our emails
  • Request complete removal from our mailing list by contacting us

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us at amman@tiakiai.com
